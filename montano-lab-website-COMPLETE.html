Udayanga Wanninayake Ph.D.
Postdoctoral Researcher

Uday is a postdoctoral fellow in Dr. Montano's Lab since August of 2025. He received his bachelors in chemistry in 2007 from University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, and PhD in organic chemistry in 2013 from Michigan State University.

He is interested in protein biochemistry, enzymology and applications in enzyme replacement therapies for lysosomal storage diseases.

His goal is to be a principle investigator in developing enzyme replacement therapies for lysosomal storage diseases. Uday hopes to establish a non-profit organization/foundation for the awareness of hemangiosarcoma in dogs.
